About Me

Hello! My name is Maryam Ahmed and I am an incoming 3rd year student at The University of Illinois at Chicago majoring in Computer Science. I lived in Toronto for five years and spent one year in India before moving to the USA when I was six.

My hobbies include reading, playing badminton, and baking. My most favorite literary series are The Inheritance Cycle by Christopher Paolini, Ranger's Apprentice by John Flanagan, and The Infernal Devices by Cassandra Clare. I am an avid fan of creating cake recipes from scratch.

One of my most treasured memories was a family trip to the countryside in Ontario, Canada. We had rented a spooky old lakehouse and had a lot of fun scaring each other. It was the perfect spot for stargazing and canoeing as well as hiking.

Someplace that I would like to visit in the future is Macau. I really want to see The House of Dancing Water show and (maybe) bungee jump off the Macau tower. Macau is also home to the world's first figure-eight ferris wheel. I hope to have the chance to take a trip to this city in the near future.
